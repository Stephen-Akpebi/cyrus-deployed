Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) section for Cyrus Consumer Finance, your go-to Buy Now, Pay Later app. We understand that you might have questions about how our app works and how it can benefit you. To assist you, we’ve compiled a list of common queries and provided detailed answers.

- Buy Now, Pay Later is a payment method that allows you to make purchases and receive your items immediately, while spreading the cost of your purchase into manageable installments. You can select the "Buy Now, Pay Later" option at checkout, and the app will handle the payment arrangement for you.

- Interest rates and fees can vary depending on the app and the specific terms of your purchase. Some apps offer interest-free options if you repay within a certain period. Be sure to check the terms and conditions for each transaction.

- Our app is widely accepted at a variety of online and physical stores, including major retailers and e-commerce platforms. You can check the app to see a list of supported merchants or simply look for the "Buy Now, Pay Later" option at checkout.

- Repayment terms depend on your app's policies and the specific purchase. Typically, you can choose from various installment options, such as weekly or monthly payments. The app will provide details on repayment schedules and amounts.

- Many Buy Now, Pay Later apps perform a quick credit assessment during the checkout process. However, this doesn't always result in a hard credit check that affects your credit score. Approval is often quick and straightforward, and even users with limited credit history may be eligible to use the app.
